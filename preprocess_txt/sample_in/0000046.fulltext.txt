LEAD: Pamela Therese Sebastian, a reporter for The Wall Street Journal, and Dr. Gerald A. Ridge, an internist in private practice in Bronxville, N.Y., were married yesterday at All Saints Episcopal Church in Manhattan. The Rev. R. DeWitt Mallary Jr. performed the ceremony. Pamela Therese Sebastian, a reporter for The Wall Street Journal, and Dr. Gerald A. Ridge, an internist in private practice in Bronxville, N.Y., were married yesterday at All Saints Episcopal Church in Manhattan. The Rev. R. DeWitt Mallary Jr. performed the ceremony. Mrs. Ridge, a graduate of Arizona State University with a master's degree in journalism from the University of Illinois, is a daughter of the late Mr. and Mrs. Victor Sebastian of Chicago Heights, Ill. Dr. Ridge, the son of Mr. and Mrs. Gerald F. Ridge of Boston, graduated from Harvard College and the University of California at San Francisco School of Medicine. His previous marriage ended in divorce, as did that of his bride.
